# Lead Magnet — Creating lead capture asset...
*Creates a lead magnet with format selection, content outline, landing page copy, email capture integration, delivery sequence, and nurture follow-up.*

## Scope Guard

Use this section to decide whether this skill is appropriate for the current task. **ACTIVE** means the skill is relevant; **DORMANT** means another skill or a general response is likely better.

| Context | Status |
|---------|--------|
| User says "lead magnet", "opt-in", "freebie", "list building" | ACTIVE |
| User says "email list growth" or "grow my list" | ACTIVE |
| User wants to create a free asset to capture emails | ACTIVE |
| User wants a full sales funnel | DORMANT — use Sales Funnel |
| User wants paid ad copy | DORMANT — use Facebook Ad or Google Ad |

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It's Wrong |
|---------|---------------|
| "Give away your best stuff" | Your best stuff is the product. The lead magnet solves ONE specific problem to prove your expertise. |
| "Make a 50-page ebook" | Long ebooks have <10% completion rate. Short, actionable assets convert better. |
| "Any free thing works" | The lead magnet must directly relate to your paid offer. Unrelated freebies attract unqualified leads. |
| "No follow-up after delivery" | The lead magnet is the start of the relationship, not the end. Nurture with 3-5 emails minimum. |
| "Ask for too much info" | Every additional form field reduces conversions by 10-15%. Name + email is enough. |

## Protocol

### Step 1: Gather Requirements

If the user hasn't provided details, ask:

> 1. **Your paid product** — what are you ultimately selling? (name, price, category)
> 2. **Target audience** — who are you trying to attract? (role, pain point, skill level)
> 3. **Biggest problem** — what's the #1 pain your audience has right now?
> 4. **Quick win** — what small result can you deliver in <15 minutes?
> 5. **Distribution** — how will you drive traffic? (ads, SEO, social, partnerships)

### Step 2: Select Lead Magnet Type

Choose based on audience preference and production effort:

| Type | Best For | Time to Create | Conversion Rate |
|------|---------|----------------|----------------|
| **Checklist** | Process-oriented audiences (devs, marketers) | 1-2 hours | Very high (35-50%) |
| **Template/Swipe file** | People who want done-for-you | 2-4 hours | Very high (35-50%) |
| **Cheat sheet** | Quick-reference audiences | 1-2 hours | High (30-40%) |
| **Mini-course (email)** | Audiences who value learning | 4-8 hours | High (25-35%) |
| **Calculator/tool** | Data-driven audiences | 8-20 hours | High (30-45%) |
| **Video training** | Visual learners, coaching audiences | 4-8 hours | Medium (20-30%) |
| **Ebook/guide** | Thought leadership positioning | 8-20 hours | Medium (15-25%) |
| **Quiz/assessment** | Entertainment-driven audiences | 8-16 hours | Very high (40-60%) |
| **Free trial/sample** | Product-led growth SaaS or physical | Varies | Medium (15-25%) |
| **Webinar recording** | B2B, high-ticket offers | 2-4 hours (if recorded) | Medium (20-30%) |

**Selection rule:** Start with checklists, templates, or cheat sheets. They're fast to create, high-converting, and easy to test. Graduate to courses and tools once you've validated the topic.

```markdown
- [ ] [Actionable item with brief explanation]
- [ ] [Actionable item with brief explanation]

## Quick Wins
[3 items they can do right now in <5 minutes]

## Next Steps
[Bridge to your paid product — "Want the complete system? Check out [Product]"]
```

**Template/swipe file structure:**

```markdown
# [Template Name] — [What It Helps Them Do]

## How to Use This Template
[3-step quick-start guide]

## Template
[The actual template — copy-paste ready]

## Customization Guide
[How to adapt it for their specific situation]

## Examples
[2-3 filled-in examples showing the template in action]

## Next Steps
[Bridge to paid product]
```

**Mini-course (email) structure:**

```
Lesson 1 (Day 0): [Quick win — immediate value]
Lesson 2 (Day 2): [Core concept — the "why"]
Lesson 3 (Day 4): [Framework — the "how"]
Lesson 4 (Day 6): [Advanced tactic — deeper skill]
Lesson 5 (Day 8): [Implementation plan + bridge to paid offer]
```

**Content rules:**
- Solve ONE specific problem (not everything about the topic)
- Deliver a quick win within the first 5 minutes of consumption
- Bridge to your paid offer naturally (not salesy — "if you want to go deeper")
- Include your branding (logo, colors, website URL)
- Format: PDF for checklists/guides, email for courses, web app for calculators

### Step 4: Write Landing Page Copy

**Landing page structure:**

```
[Above the fold]
Headline: [Specific outcome they'll get]
Subheadline: [How — "The [type] that helps you [outcome] in [timeframe]"]
Hero image: Mockup of the lead magnet (PDF cover, template preview, etc.)
Form: [Name] + [Email] + [CTA Button]
Social proof: "Downloaded by 2,000+ [audience type]"

[Below the fold — optional for higher-value magnets]
What's inside: 3-5 bullet points of what they'll learn/get
Who it's for: "[Audience type] who wants to [outcome]"
About you: 2-sentence credibility statement
Testimonial: Quote from someone who used the resource
```

**Headline formulas:**
- "The [Type] That Helps You [Outcome] in [Timeframe]"
- "[Number]-Step [Type] to [Outcome] (Without [Pain Point])"
- "Free [Type]: How to [Outcome] Even If [Objection]"
- "Get the Exact [Type] I Used to [Impressive Result]"

**CTA button text (not "Submit"):**
- "Download the Free Checklist"
- "Get Instant Access"
- "Send Me the Template"
- "Start the Free Course"

**Form design rules:**
- Fields: Name + Email only (2 fields maximum)
- Button color: Contrasting color to the page
- Below button: "No spam. Unsubscribe anytime."
- No navigation links on the landing page (remove header/footer nav)

### Step 5: Set Up Delivery Sequence

**Email 1 — Delivery (immediate):**

```
Subject: Here's your [lead magnet name]
Preview: Download it now — plus a quick tip to get started

Hi [Name],

Here's the [lead magnet name] you requested:

 [Download Link / Access Link]

Quick tip to get the most out of it:
[One actionable sentence — what to do first]

I'll send you a few more tips over the next week to help you
[achieve the outcome]. Keep an eye out.

[Your name]
[Company]

P.S. Hit reply and tell me — what's your biggest challenge with
[topic]? I read every response.
```

**Email 2 — Value add (Day 2):**

```
Subject: [Quick tip related to lead magnet topic]
Preview: This takes 5 minutes and makes a big difference

Hi [Name],

Did you get a chance to use the [lead magnet]?

Here's a quick tip that most people miss:
[2-3 paragraph teaching moment — delivers standalone value]

Try this today — it takes less than 5 minutes.

[Your name]

P.S. Tomorrow I'll share [tease next email's topic].
```

**Email 3 — Bridge to offer (Day 4-5):**

```
Subject: The next step after [lead magnet topic]
Preview: [Lead magnet] handles the basics — here's how to go further

Hi [Name],

The [lead magnet] gives you a solid foundation for [outcome].

But if you want to [bigger outcome / save more time / go deeper],
you'll need [what your paid product provides].

That's exactly why I built [Product Name]:
→ [Benefit 1]
→ [Benefit 2]
→ [Benefit 3]

[CTA: Check out [Product Name] →]

No pressure — the [lead magnet] works great on its own.
But if you want the complete system, this is the next step.

[Your name]
```

### Step 6: Optimization Checklist

**Landing page optimization:**
- [ ] Page loads in <3 seconds
- [ ] Mobile responsive (50%+ traffic is mobile)
- [ ] Single CTA — no competing links
- [ ] Headline promises a specific, desirable outcome
- [ ] Lead magnet mockup is visible above the fold
- [ ] Social proof present (download count, testimonials)
- [ ] Privacy note below form ("No spam. Unsubscribe anytime.")
- [ ] Thank-you page confirms delivery and sets expectations

**Lead magnet content:**
- [ ] Delivers a quick win in <5 minutes
- [ ] Solves ONE specific problem (not everything)
- [ ] Branded with logo, colors, and URL
- [ ] Bridges to paid offer naturally (last page/section)
- [ ] Professional design (even basic Canva templates help)

**Email sequence:**
- [ ] Delivery email sends within 2 minutes of signup
- [ ] Download link works and isn't behind a login wall
- [ ] 3-email minimum follow-up (value, value, bridge)
- [ ] Unsubscribe link in every email
- [ ] Reply-to set to a monitored address

**Tracking:**
- [ ] Landing page conversion rate tracked (target: 30%+)
- [ ] Email open rates tracked (target: 40%+ for delivery email)
- [ ] Click-through to paid offer tracked
- [ ] Cost per lead calculated (if using paid traffic)
